ABOUT BAGUIO CITY

Known as the "City of Pines," Baguio City is a mountain haven of cool breezes, lush green landscapes filled with pine trees, and rich cultural heritage. Located high in the Cordillera Mountains, Baguio City offers numerous attractions, activities, and local wares for you to discover.

EVENTS

Baguio City hosts an exciting array of events year-round, including the vibrant Panagbenga Flower Festival that fills the streets with color. With cultural celebrations, food fairs, and more, every visit promises a unique and unforgettable experience. Check out the Baguio Tourism Facebook group to stay updated!
