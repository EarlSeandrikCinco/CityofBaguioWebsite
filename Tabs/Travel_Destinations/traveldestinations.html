TRAVEL DESTINATIONS

HERE ARE THREE OF THE MOST POPULAR TRAVEL DESTINATIONS IN THE CITY OF PINES:

BURNHAM PARK

Officially called the Burnham Park Reservation, is a historic urban park situated in the heart of downtown Baguio, Philippines. Positioned along Jose Abad Santos Drive, it provides a peaceful retreat from the bustle of the city. Spanning 32 hectares, the park features a range of attractions, including a children's playground, a rose garden, an orchidarium, a picnic area, and a traditional Igorot garden.

CAMP JOHN HAY

Camp John Hay, a peaceful retreat in the City of Pines, offers a mix of relaxation and excitement. With plenty of leisure spots, activity centers, shops, and dining options, there's something enjoyable in every corner. The beautiful views of tall pine trees and green mountains make it even more appealing.

BAGUIO CATHEDRAL OF OUR LADY OF THE ATONEMENT

The Our Lady of the Atonement Cathedral, more commonly referred to as Baguio Cathedral, is a Roman Catholic church and the seat of the Diocese of Baguio in the Philippines. It is situated at Cathedral Loop, near Session Road in Baguio. The cathedral, dedicated to the Blessed Virgin Mary under the title Our Lady of Atonement, is well-known for its unique architecture, featuring twin spires and stained glass windows, making it a popular tourist spot in the city.
