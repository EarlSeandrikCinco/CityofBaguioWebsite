LOCAL WARES

LOOKING FOR PASALUBONG TO TAKE HOME? THE CITY OF PINES HAS YOU COVERED!

❮ ❯

Ube and Strawberry Jams


Baguio offers delicious ube and strawberry jams, perfect for bringing a taste of the city home. Made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients, these jams highlight the vibrant flavors of the region.

Handwoven Fabrics


Discover the intricate handwoven fabrics of Baguio, made by skilled artisans. These fabrics not only make great souvenirs but also help support the local economy and preserve traditional weaving techniques.

Wood Carvings


These carvings, often made from indigenous wood like narra and mahogany, showcase the artistry and cultural heritage of the area.
